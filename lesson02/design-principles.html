Rule of Thirds

https://www.staak.co.uk/

This London-based design studio has implemented the rule of thirds really well in their web page design. Using the grid, they have determined where there should be negative space and where to place their attention-grabbing text. The eye naturally travels from the more graphic title text to the subtle descriptive text on the bottom left.

Visual Hierarchy

https://www.netflix.com/

In my opinion the good example of visual hierarchy is Netflix website because bigger elements get the most attention and are deemed more important.

White Space and Clean Design

https://www.apple.com/uk/macbook-air/

The good examle of white space and Cleaning Design is Apple.com. As one of the early adopters of using whitespace on the web to showcase a product, Apple set the standard for building breathing room into their design. In this example Apple placing imagery strategically to draw the users eye to specific elements down the page.
